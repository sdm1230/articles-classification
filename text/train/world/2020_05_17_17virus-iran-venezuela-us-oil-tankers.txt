Iran Summons Ambassador Over Possible U.S. Threat to Venezuela Shipment
By ReutersIran summoned the Swiss ambassador, who represents U.S. interests in the Islamic Republic, over possible measures the United States could take against an Iranian fuel shipment to Venezuela, the Mehr news agency reported.A senior official in U.S. President Donald Trump’s administration told Reuters on Thursday the United States was considering measures it could take in response to Iran’s shipment of fuel to crisis-stricken Venezuela.
