Where Do You Go for a Walk?
By The New York TimesGatherings are banned. Restaurants are closed. Concerts are canceled. States across the country have issued shelter-in-place orders, and illnesses and job losses continue to grow. Many Americans are spending most of their time at home with few options left to distract them from the relentless drumbeat of bad news, except for maybe a walk outside.Where do you go to escape? We want to hear how you are using time outdoors as a balm or a distraction from the pandemic. Where do you go for a walk, what do you see, and how does it make you feel?We may assign a photographer to go on a walk, with an eye toward capturing images of the feelings evoked in your stories.
